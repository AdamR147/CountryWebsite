Welcome to the Dominican Republic!

Home to beautiful beaches, resorts, & more!

Dominican Republic is located in the Caribbean, on the island of Hispaniola. Dominican Republic is the second largest island in the Caribbean with the first being Cuba. Dominican Republic declared itself independent on February 27th, 1844. Dominican Republic is also the only country in the world to have the Holy Bible on the flag. Dominican Republic streets feature Spanish language signs, the official language of the country. The Dominican Republic neighboring countries are Haiti, where they share a border, followed by Puerto Rico and Cuba. The capital city in Dominican Republic is Santo Domingo where it features many historical landmarks such as The Cathedral of Santa Maria la Menor, which is one of the very first Cathedrals to be built in the Americas. Another famous landmark located in Santo Domingo is Alcazar de Colon. Dominican Republic is also known for a variety of things such as Merengue, a style of dance. Another thing that is also common in Dominican Republic is baseball, one of the most popular sports. Another fun fact is that Dominican Republic attracts a vast majority of tourists as it the most visited country in the Caribbean. The Dominican Republic also features the largest economy in the Carribean and also is recognized as the seventh largest economy in Latin America. The economy has been rapidly increasing for 25 years in the Western Hemisphere, featuring a average real GDP growth rate of 5.3% between 1992 and 2018. Another thing Dominican Republic is known for is it's hot climates, where the average temperature is 78.8 degrees fahrenheight which increase livelihood and can also be a good thing for growing crops. One of the most grown crops in Dominican Republic is sugarcane, a major traditional export, alongside bananas and plantain. Dominican Republic is one of the most diverse nations in the world and it has a variety to offer.
